Free Internet Service Offers

With the shift to remote work and distance learning due to COVID-19, it's more important than ever to have reliable internet access. Many internet service providers (ISPs) have rolled out special offers for students (usually two months of free service for new subscribers).

It's important to note that in order to take advantage of any particular offer, you must live in an area served by the ISP.

Comcast Internet Essentials

"New Internet Essentials customers will receive two free months of Internet service, which is available to all qualified low-income households for $9.95/month plus tax. Apply by April 30, 2020."

They define low-income as "eligible for public assistance programs like the National School Lunch Program, housing assistance, Medicaid, SNAP, SSI, and others.""

Apply at www.internetessentials.com/covid19

Optimum Altice Internet

"For households with K-12 and/or college students who may be displaced due to school closures and who do not currently have home internet access, we are offering our Altice Advantage Internet solution for free for 60 days to any new customer household within our footprint."

Apply at www.alticeadvantageinternet.com

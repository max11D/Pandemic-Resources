Resources for the COVID‑19 Pandemic

This website was born out of a huge pile of resources the creators put together to help their friends through the COVID‑19 pandemic. This is meant to be a better organized version of the brain dump that was/is our Discord "resources" channel.

This was put together by (and primarily for) students at Queens College of the City University of New York (CUNY), but it may still be of use to anyone, anywhere.

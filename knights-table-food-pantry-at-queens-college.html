Knights Table Food Pantry at Queens College

"The Knights Table Food Pantry is committed to promoting health and well-being by providing nourishment to all Queens College students. We aim to alleviate the presence of hunger and food insecurity in the lives of our students, and their families."

Important: As of March 20th, 2020 the pantry's websites contain outdated information. Hours are incorrect and the websites do not mention that it's currently by appointment only. We strongly recommend checking their social media pages (which are updated more regularly) and calling ahead in order to avoid inconvenience.

First, apply at https://bit.ly/QCKnightsTableApplication

Second, make an appointment at https://bit.ly/QCKnightsTableAppointment

Hours are Mon - Thu, 12:00 - 4:00 pm
